1One small step at a time — never everything at once.
2Just tell me what you did in plain words — I’ll polish it.
3About 5 minutes. Pause anytime — your progress is saved.

What do you do, in a few words?

This is the short line at the very top of your resume — who you are at a glance. Don’t overthink it, and feel free to skip if you’re not sure.

A few examples: “Registered Nurse, 8 years in pediatrics” · “Marketing generalist who loves data” · “Recent grad, eager to learn”
